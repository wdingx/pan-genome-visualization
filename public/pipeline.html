About panXpanX is a powerful interactive platform for pan-genome analysis and exploration. It provides multiple different views on annotated genomes and allows rapid search by gene name, diversity, gene gain and loss event, etc. Strain-specific meta-data is integrated into the phylogenetic tree viewer such that associations between gene presence and phenotypes (resistance,virulence,etc) can be easily spotted. Comments, suggestions for improvements, bug reports etc are welcome.
